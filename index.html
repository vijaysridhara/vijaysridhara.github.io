Image Librarian

The Image manager

Organize your images (Jpg, png) in searchable and tagged format. This works indestructively without writing any information on the images. It maintains a separate cache of small thumbnails for your search, tagging, comments and rating of images. Beauty is that you need not disturb your source folders of images, but organize them in ImageLibrarian, the way you want them to be.
